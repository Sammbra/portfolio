Brutalism

This project is a poster series inspired by brutalist design and some of my favorite music artists. I was drawn to brutalism for its raw, direct approach bold typography, strong contrast, and layouts that prioritize structure over polish. I used this style as a way to step outside my usual visual comfort zone and focus heavily on layout and type. The challenge was to translate the energy and identity of each artist into a harsh, stripped-back visual language, and bring those differences together into a cohesive series.

Project overview

Date: October, 2025
Scope: Poster design, Photo Editing
Programs: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

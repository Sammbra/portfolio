Cities

As I’ve mentioned before, I love exploring. This project features a collection of posters inspired by the cities I’ve visited, combining my own photography with graphic design. I chose images that, in my view, best capture each place’s character and atmosphere. Throughout the process, I focused on improving my photo editing skills and experimenting with different design techniques. The final result is a set of visuals that represent each city in a way that feels personal, honest, and easy to understand.

Project overview

Date: September, 2025
Scope: Poster design
Photo Editing Programs: Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop
